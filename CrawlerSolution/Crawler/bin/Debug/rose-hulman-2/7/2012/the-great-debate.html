Aspirational vision. It's in the Rose-Hulman DNA. It's in our history.

Through strategic planning, we successfully launched an evolutionary era in 1976 with "The Blueprint for Excellence." Then, in 1995 thanks to "The Vision to Be the Best," we launched a golden age, during which we've held national #1 status for 13 years. And now we are poised for another leap, whereby Rose-Hulman moves from "best-in-class" to "great."

"Great" goes beyond superlatives, beyond "best." Best competes, edging out peers; "great" aspires, stepping out, peerless. "Great" makes history by changing the course of the future.

It is incumbent upon Rose-Hulman, as leaders who have spent 13 years at the top of engineering education, to set the pace for the education of the future. That visionary future is a moving target, and to hit it, we must aim ahead of the curve. When we tap the extraordinary minds of the greater Rose-Hulman community, we will aim true.

In our process towards "great," we are asking every member of the Rose-Hulman Community - those who have passed through our doors; those who work, teach and learn here; those who have built or felt our impact - to set our collective minds on this challenge: What strategies and structures, what commitments, will help us fulfill Rose-Hulman's promise to be "great" in the 21st century?

Join the process. Monitor the progress and feedback from our community as we gather ideas and gain momentum on our path to "great."
