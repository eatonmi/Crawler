You can expect a personal approach to education at Rose-Hulman

Our 11:1 student-faculty ratio means professors can take a personal interest in each student's success at Rose-Hulman. Find more fast facts like majors, student organizations and athletics.

Welcome to Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology, home to 1,800 undergraduate and 100 graduate students majoring in engineering, science or mathematics. As we prepare students for their careers, our emphasis is on a hands-on education where students learn the value of teamwork as well as formulas and equations.

Top National Rankings
It's true Rose-Hulman has been ranked No. 1 by U.S. News & World Report among institutions whose highest degree in engineering is the master's. With 99% placement, the school is ranked in the Payscale top 10 for starting salaries. Rose-Hulman is recognized with many more national accolades.
