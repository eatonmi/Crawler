Welcome to the Rose-Hulman Athletics Department

Rose-Hulman student-athletes excel in 21 NCAA Division III men's and women's varsity sports.  For 26 consecutive years, at least one Rose-Hulman athlete has been earned Academic All-American honors to mark the sixth longest streak among all United States colleges.

Rose-Hulman's athletic facilities are among the nation's best at the NCAA Division III level.  The Fightin' Engineers have hosted the 2007 and 2009 NCAA Division III Indoor Track and Field National Championships, the 2002 and 2003 NCAA Division III Women's Basketball Final Four and the 2005, 2006 and 2008 NCAA Division III Mideast Baseball Regionals due to the quality of facilities and people within the athletic department.
